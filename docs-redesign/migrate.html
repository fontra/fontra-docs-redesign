The zero-risk way to try Fontra

You don't have to commit to anything. Fontra reads your native files and reloads automatically when they change on disk:

  1. Drop your .glyphs, .designspace or .ufo onto Fontra Pak.
  2. Keep working in your usual editor. Save.
  3. Watch Fontra update, live — a synchronized second view.
Why this matters

You can evaluate Fontra on a real project, on a second monitor, with no conversion and no risk. Keep your current tool as the source of truth until you're convinced.
